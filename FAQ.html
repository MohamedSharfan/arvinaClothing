Frequently asked questions

1. How do I make a purchase?

To make a purchase, click the 'order now' button and proceed with further directions given by the website.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We only accept cash on delivery (COD), bank payments,online bank transfer,KOKO,Mintpay,VISA and MasterCard.

3. An item I want to buy is sold out will you be restocking it?

Restocking our styles depends on its demand. If the style is frequently asked about, we will be restocking it within a few weeks. If not, you can pick a similar style using the 'search' feature.

4. Can I ship to multiple addresses?

We ship to one address in one delivery, although you can add a different address as the recipient's address.

5. How long does delivery take, and what are the charges?

Our delivery duration is 3-5 business days. The delivery charges depend on the recipient's location.

6. What is the return notice period?

Any purchase you make will not be eligible to exchange after 7 days. In order to exchange, the garment should be in its original condition and it will only be accepted with its receipt.

7.How do I cancel my order?

You can inform us throught email,call, or through facebook. Please be kind enough to inform us within 24 hours after placing the order since the product will be reserved for you.The cancellation should happen within 24 hours of placing the order.

8.How can I track my order?

When you make a purchase, an email will be sent to you with your order details and it will consist a link that you can click on to track your order

9.Can I change or amend my order once it has been place?

Once an order is placed you will not be able to change or amend the order.

10.Any more questions?

Feel free to contact us our hotline or send us an email, we will be happy to assist you
